Chemistry MCQs: Experimental Design

Question 1: Which apparatus is most appropriate for measuring time in a chemical experiment?

A) Burette
B) Stopwatch
C) Measuring cylinder
D) Gas syringe

Answer: B
Explanation: A stopwatch is specifically designed to measure time intervals accurately in experiments.

Question 2: Which apparatus would you use to measure temperature in an experiment?

A) Thermometer
B) Pipette
C) Balance
D) Burette

Answer: A
Explanation: A thermometer is used to measure temperature accurately.

Question 3: To measure the mass of a substance accurately, you would use a:

D) Gas syringe

Answer: C
Explanation: A balance is used to measure the mass of a substance accurately.
